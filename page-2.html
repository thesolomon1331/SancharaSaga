About Nethravathi Trek


  • Nestled within the enchanting Kudremukh forest range, the Nethravathi trek stands as a hidden gem, offering both beauty and adventure to intrepid travelers. Immerse yourself in the emerald hues of the Western Ghats, where the lesser-known path unfolds. Keep an eye out for the graceful Sambar Deers, majestic Bisons, and elusive Bears that call this wilderness home. Embark on your journey from Bangalore, indulge in comfortable camp stays, relish delightful meals, and create lasting memories as you traverse the picturesque trails with your companions.

    • Explore the Nethravathi Trek with these exclusive details:


    • Limited Slots: Typically, we keep the trip size intimate, ranging from 10 to 20 participants.
    • Culinary Delights: Enjoy 2 hearty breakfasts, 1 sumptuous lunch, and 1 flavorful dinner - all vegetarian and crafted in local home-based style.
    • Trek Difficulty: Moderate, ensuring both novices and enthusiasts can relish the experience.
    • Accommodation: Cozy tent stays on a sharing basis, fostering a sense of camaraderie.
    • Trip Duration: A 2-day, 2-night escapade in the mesmerizing Western Ghats near Kalasa, Chickmagalur, Karnataka.
    • Trek Distance: Approximately 12 km, unveiling stunning grasslands, thick forests, ever-flowing streams, and challenging inclinations.
    • Location: Immerse yourself in the wonders of the Western Ghats, a natural marvel near Kalasa, Chickmagalur.
    • Trail Type: Traverse through a diverse terrain, encompassing expansive grasslands, dense forests, babbling streams, and steep inclinations.

    Join us for an unforgettable adventure that blends nature, camaraderie, and the thrill of exploration.
